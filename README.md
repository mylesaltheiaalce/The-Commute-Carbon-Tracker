The-Commute-Carbon-Tracker

1. Problem Analysis
User: Alex, 29 - Sustainability - Focused Professional

Pain Point: Alex wants to live sustainably but is unsure if biking or walking actually makes a difference. He needs a simple way to see his environmental impact in numbers to stay motivated.

Value Delivered by Solution

Fast Logging: Allows users to record a commute in less than 30 seconds
Clear impact: Shows exactly how much CO2 is saved compared to driving
Motivation: Display statistics and history to encourage consistent eco-friendly choices

2. Design & Technical Approach
Tools & Technologies Used
HTML: For structuring the user interface and content
CSS: For styling, layout, gradients, and responsive design
JavaScript: For logic, calculations and interactivity
